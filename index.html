About Me

I got my start in Web Development while I was playing in my band, SpaceWaster. We were gigging professionally, traveling the country and trying to establish a prescence on social media.

At one point we figured that it would be great to have an official website so we could have all of our content tied to a single link that could fit on a sticker or a business card. Being an independent band that took pride in our DIY ethos, I decided to take charge of building and deploying the website.

At first I figured I would just pruchase a fancy WYSIWYG editor or Wordpress theme and call it a day, but being an artist, it was imperitive that our website match the attitude and the aesthetic that we put forth in our work, both on our abum covers and sonically in our music. I desired more control, so I decided to teach myself to do it from scratch. I began reading books on HTML, CSS, Javascript and began to learn what went on under the hood of Wordpress.

The result was a custom built Wordpress theme that inserted blog style posts in my own custom CSS layout. This setup allowed us to write blog posts and deploy them quickly, while maintaining the advantages of a custom built website.

As a web developer my main approach is to use frameworks and CMS editors when convenient, but never shy away from hand coding things, and looking under the hood when necessary. I am currently available for freelance work, contract hiring and full time employment.
