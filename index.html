About Me

I have been a web developer for years now. I have some freelance work under my belt and am currently taking classes to become a more full stack developer.

I got my start in Web Development while I was playing in my band, SpaceWaster. We were gigging professionally, traveling the country and trying to establish a prescence on social media.

At one point we figured that it would be great to have an official website so we could have all of our content tied to a single link that could fit on a sticker or a business card. Being an independent band that took pride in our DIY ethos, I decided to take charge of building and deploying the website.

After about 6 months of learning HTML, CSS, Wordpress and a bit of PHP I had a Wordpress site that I built from my own custom theme. I decided to continue to teach myself and pick up some freelance work on the side.

Right now I am in school for Web Development to futher my journey into full stack web development. I am in an intensive program at Penn that will teach me both front and back end skills. I am still available for freelance work and part time contracting.

On my free time I enjoy hiking, board games, dungeons and dragons, heavy metal music, and bike riding. I love traveling and have been to 43 of all 50 states.
